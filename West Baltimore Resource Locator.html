West Baltimore Resource Map




This Resource Locator focuses on the following neighborhoods:

  • Poppleton

  • Sandtown-Winchester

  • Upton



These resources are all public health related and are intended to aide in prevention and treatment of illness and disease.
As the population continues to grow and the hunt for limited resources increases, this Resource Locator is here to facilitate the process of sustaining a healthy lifestyle.



This website is designed to help you locate direct community resources that pertain to nutrition, health services, and education.





* Filters and icons are available to show resources by category
* Right clicking the resource will open an Information Window.
This will provide you with the specific address, contact information, service(s) provided, and website and photo (if available).
